Finished Paper

Even though there has been a continued focus on digitization, India’s demand for paper is expected to rise by 53 percent by 2020.

Paper is used across a number of workplaces and industries, and this is why the demand for paper and paper products is always higher than the supply. Therefore, there is a recurring need for delivering quality finished paper to meet these growing demands.

Paper is a natural and renewable product, and is 100% recyclable, which ensures the capability of satisfying future requirements through sustainable measures.

At KNAM, we supply fine and finished paper, both in reels and sheets, to different industries as per their requirement and budget.

E-Waste

Electronic waste or e-waste is a term coined for electronic products that have become obsolete or non-working, and have been discarded. E-waste includes rejected computer systems, motherboards, mobile phones and its accessories, compact discs, (LCD)/ Plasma televisions, air conditioners, etc.

India is among the top five countries in e-waste production, according to ASSOCHAM-NEC study (source). When e-waste gets accumulated in landfills, toxic substances like lead, mercury and cadmium leak into the soil and water.
